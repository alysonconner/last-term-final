Barbers

Carlos Santos

Carlos 15 year career began in a barber shop on the west-side of Ottawa, under a well-respected veteran of the trade. After refining his skills in a few of Ottawa's shops, this award-winning stylist decided to open up a shop of his own. He opened HairFellas doors to provide exceptional grooming services for men in a unique atmosphere geared specifically towards men.

"Just good conversation and great haircuts."

Natalie Lacroix

Natalie grew up in the barber shop. Her father was a trade veteran in Victoria, BC, where she got her start. She learned young, with her first paying job in the shop at 8 years old, and she never stopped loving it. Although she never took any formal training, Natalie is a pro in her field using the skills passed down from her pops help her to excel as a female traditional barber in a male-dominated career.

"I don't have any beauty shop memories. I remember the barber shop."

Paulos Garcia

Paulos is a hometown Ottawa boy - born and raised. Paulos is a long-time fan of the barber culture, and loves working in an environment that allows him to polish the art of conversation with so many new people. When he's not rocking haircuts and shaves, he's a passionate foodie and can be spotted in one of Ottawa's many restaurants.

"Being a barber is about taking care of the people."

James Bauer

James' took barber studies for 2 years before starting his career by opening a make-shift shop in his garage. After discovering that his buddies needed a reliable cut at a great price and a convenient location, James began his entrepreneurship. He worked part-time in a few local shops along the way as well as continuing his formal training as a barber through trade shows and workshops.

“Never let the truth get in the way of a good story.”
